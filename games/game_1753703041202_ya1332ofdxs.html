# Game Design Document: Harvest Rush ## Expanded Theme **"Harvest Rush: Master of the Fields"** - A fast-paced farm management teaser where players must quickly harvest crops while managing resources and weather challenges. The game reveals whether you're a Strategic Planner, Reactive Survivor, or Balanced Cultivator based on your farming decisions under pressure. ## Core Gameplay Loop 1. **Goal**: Harvest as many crops as possible before time runs out 2. **Challenge**: Crops grow at different rates, weather events threaten harvests, limited tools 3. **Feedback**: Visual crop growth, particle effects on harvest, score updates, weather warnings 4. **Reward**: Points, combo multipliers, personality insights ## Game Systems ### Physics & Interactions - Crop growth animations with scaling and color transitions - Weather particle effects (rain, sun rays, wind) - Tool selection with visual feedback - Harvest animations with satisfying particle bursts ### Progression (90 seconds) - **0-30s**: Tutorial phase, slow crop growth, gentle weather - **30-60s**: Medium difficulty, faster growth, occasional weather events - **60-90s**: High intensity, rapid growth, frequent weather challenges ### Behavioral Tracking - **Reaction Time**: Speed of crop harvesting decisions - **Planning vs Reactive**: Early harvests vs waiting for full growth - **Risk Taking**: Harvesting during weather events - **Efficiency**: Tool switching frequency and timing - **Persistence**: Continued engagement during difficult moments ### Art & Animation - Vibrant green fields with animated grass - Colorful crops that grow and sway - Dynamic weather effects - Breathing UI elements - Smooth tool selection animations ## Personality Mapping - **Strategic Planner**: High efficiency, good timing, low reaction variance - **Bold Risk-Taker**: Harvests during storms, high action frequency - **Steady Cultivator**: Consistent harvesting, balanced approach - **Reactive Survivor**: High reaction times, adapts to weather quickly --- # Implementation ```json { "gameTitle": "Harvest Rush", "file": { "fileName": "index.html", "contentType": "text/html", "encoding": "utf-8", "content": "\n\n\n \n \n \n \n Harvest Rush\n \n\n\n
