# Game Design Document: "Harvest Rush" ## Expanded Theme **"Harvest Rush: Master of the Fields"** - A fast-paced farm management teaser where players must quickly harvest crops while managing resources and weather challenges. The game reveals whether you're a Strategic Planner, Reactive Survivor, or Balanced Farmer based on your decision-making patterns. ## Core Gameplay Loop 1. **Goal**: Harvest as many crops as possible before time runs out 2. **Challenge**: Balance speed vs. efficiency while managing water and avoiding crop diseases 3. **Feedback**: Visual score updates, particle effects, screen shake on successful harvests 4. **Reward**: Unlocking new crop types and personality insights ## Game Systems - **Physics**: Smooth crop growth animations and water droplet effects - **Particles**: Harvest sparkles, water splashes, disease spread effects - **UI**: Breathing score counter, animated progress bars, floating damage numbers - **Input**: Tap/click to harvest, drag to water crops - **Analytics**: Track reaction time, planning vs reactive behavior, risk tolerance ## Progression - **0-20s**: Tutorial phase with slow-growing basic crops - **20-60s**: Multiple crop types, weather events introduced - **60-90s**: Disease outbreaks, resource scarcity challenges - **90s+**: Rapid-fire decision making with maximum complexity ## Behavioral Tracking - **Reaction Time**: Average time between crop readiness and harvest - **Planning Score**: Ratio of proactive watering vs reactive responses - **Risk Tolerance**: Willingness to let crops grow longer for bonus points - **Persistence**: Continued engagement during difficult phases ## Art & Animation Style - Vibrant cartoon farm aesthetic with constant subtle motion - Multi-layered backgrounds with animated clouds and swaying grass - Bold, contrasting colors that intensify with difficulty - Smooth GSAP-powered transitions and UI breathing effects --- # Implementation ```json { "gameTitle": "Harvest Rush", "file": { "fileName": "index.html", "contentType": "text/html", "encoding": "utf-8", "content": "\n\n\n \n \n \n \n Harvest Rush\n \n\n\n
